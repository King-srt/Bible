6. KONTEKS LOKASI (PENTING - WAJIB DIIKUTI)

* Jika terdapat lokasi (kota, sungai, gunung, wilayah), WAJIB tambahkan penjelasan singkat
* Penjelasan harus natural dan menyatu dalam narasi
* Jangan seperti ensiklopedia, tetap storytelling

Contoh:

* "the Jordan River, a significant river where many important events took place"
* "the Sea of Galilee, a large lake in northern Israel where Jesus often taught"
* "Mount Sinai, the mountain where Moses received God's commandments"
