# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Bible Story App ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App processes, why it is used, and how it is handled.

## 1. Data We Process

The App may process the following data:

- Anonymous Firebase Authentication identifier
  - Used to create an anonymous session so your bookmarks can be associated with your device session.
- Bookmark data
  - Stories you save as bookmarks are stored in Firebase Firestore under your anonymous user ID.
- App content data
  - Stories and characters are downloaded from Firebase Firestore and cached locally on your device for faster access and offline reading.

## 2. Data We Do Not Intentionally Collect

The App does not intentionally collect:

- Your real name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Payment information
- Precise location

## 3. Why We Use Data

We use the processed data to:

- keep your bookmark list available in the app
- synchronize bookmark data for your anonymous session
- load and update story and character content
- improve app reliability and content delivery

## 4. Storage and Third-Party Services

The App uses Google Firebase services:

- Firebase Authentication
- Cloud Firestore

These services may store data on servers operated by Google.

The App also stores cached story and character content locally on your device to improve performance and offline access.

## 5. Data Retention

- Bookmark data may remain stored while your anonymous session remains active.
- Local cached content remains on your device until the app is cleared, reinstalled, or local app data is removed.

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is designed as a Bible story and character reading app. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond the anonymous technical data described above.

## 7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data through Firebase platform security and application-level access controls. However, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

## 8. Your Choices

You may stop using the App at any time.
You may also remove local app data by clearing the app storage or uninstalling the App.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
